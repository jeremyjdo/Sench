# Ornikar - Scout Report
*2025-02-02*
---
## Overview
Ornikar is France's leading online driving school, founded in 2013. Digital-first platform connecting learners with certified independent instructors, offering driving education, auto insurance, and mobility services at 30% lower cost.
| | |
|---|---|
| Founded | 2013 |
| Stage | Series C |
| Funding | €146M total |
| Headcount | ~250 |
| HQ | Paris, France |
---
## Insights
- First 100% online driving school in France with 5M+ students served
- Reached €100M revenue in 2024 (€80M driving + €20M insurance)
- 1,800+ certified instructors across 2,800+ meeting points
- Successfully pivoted to insurance (100,000+ policyholders, 4.7/5)
