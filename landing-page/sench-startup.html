## Product
### Core Product Overview
Ornikar is a digital-first driving school platform that disrupts traditional auto-école models by connecting learners directly with certified independent instructors. The platform handles the entire learning journey—from theory (code de la route) to practical lessons and exam booking—while leveraging technology to reduce costs by 30-50% compared to traditional schools.
### Major Product Components
1. Code de la Route (Theory): AI-powered learning platform with 1,500+ exam-compliant questions, progress tracking, and unlimited practice tests. Mobile-first design enables learning anywhere.
2. Practical Driving Lessons: Marketplace connecting 1,800+ certified instructors with learners. Flexible scheduling, geolocation-based matching across 2,800+ meeting points.
3. Exam Booking: Automated exam slot reservation system, reducing wait times. Accelerated programs available in Paris & Nantes.
4. Ornikar Assurance Auto: Full-stack insurance product for new drivers (100,000+ policyholders). Leverages driving data for personalized pricing. 4.7/5 rating.
5. Ornikar Assurance Habitation: Home insurance cross-sell targeting young renters, starting at €2.17/month.
6. B2B Services: Point recovery courses (2,000+ programs), CPF financing integration, and road safety training for businesses.
7. International Expansion: Onroad brand for Spanish market, same tech stack localized.
