# Luxury Resale - Value Chain Analysis
*2025-02-02*
---
## Executive Summary
The luxury resale market encompasses all activities involved in the secondary circulation of luxury goods, from authentication to logistics to final sale. The chain is characterized by fragmentation at the sourcing step, high margin concentration at the platform/retail level, and significant friction in authentication and pricing. Global market estimated at €30-40B with 12-15% CAGR.

## Chain Summary
| Step | Primary Actors | Margin Share | Digitization |
|------|----------------|--------------|--------------|
| Sourcing | Individuals, estate sales | 5% | Low |
| Authentication | Entrupy, in-house teams | 8% | Medium |

| Listing & Sale | Vestiaire, The RealReal | 45% | High |
| Fulfillment | 3PLs, white-glove services | 15% | Medium |
| After-sales | The Restory, repair shops | 20% | Low |
---
## Step Analysis: Authentication
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Key Players | Entrupy (AI), Real Authentication, platform in-house |
| Market Structure | Semi-consolidated around 3-4 tech providers |
| Margin Capture | 8% of total value |
| Entry Barriers | High - requires expertise and trust |
### Trends
- AI-powered authentication gaining traction
- Blockchain certificates for provenance (experimental)
- Brands exploring official authentication programs
### Frictions
- Speed vs accuracy tradeoff
- Cost per item ($5-30) limits lower-value goods
- Counterfeits becoming more sophisticated
### Opportunity Signals
- Real-time mobile authentication for C2C
- Authentication-as-a-service API
- Brand-certified pre-owned programs
