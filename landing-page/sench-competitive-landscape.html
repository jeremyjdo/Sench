# Expense Management - Competitive Landscape
*2025-02-02*
---
## Executive Summary
The expense management market exhibits an oligopoly structure in enterprise (top 5 players ~70% share) but remains fragmented in SMB. Key value gaps exist in vertical-specific solutions, emerging market coverage, and AI-native expense automation. US and UK markets most mature with clear PLG patterns emerging.
---

| Top 5 Share | ~65% of enterprise market |

## Inspirational Players
### Ramp (USA)
Why inspirational: Redefined value prop from "manage expenses" to "save money"
Funding: $1.6B raised, $8.1B valuation
Transferable lessons:
- Lead with ROI, not features
- AI as core differentiator
- PLG with enterprise overlay
