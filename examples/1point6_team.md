# 1POINT6 - Team Analysis

*2025-02-02*

---

## Summary

1POINT6 is a French fintech specializing in payment infrastructure for marketplaces, co-created by BNP Paribas and startup studio 321 in October 2023. The team has grown to approximately 15-19 members with a strong focus on engineering, compliance, and commercial functions. The co-founders bring deep fintech expertise from HiPay, Intuit, and multiple entrepreneurial ventures, backed by the stability of Europe's largest banking group.

## Founders

### Guillaume Massis - CEO & Co-Founder

**Background:**
Guillaume Massis brings extensive experience in business development and go-to-market strategies. Before co-founding 1POINT6, he served as Chief Sales Officer at Intuit France for two years. His career spans multiple ventures including Panto (1POINT6's predecessor), Atlanticus Music, and FitCam Health. He holds degrees from KEDGE Business School (1996-2000) and the University of Cambridge.

**Background:**

A serial entrepreneur, he has co-founded multiple technology companies across fintech, digital television, and mobile telecommunications sectors. ## Analysis

### Composition

- **Total identified:** 19 on LinkedIn
- **LinkedIn company range:** 11-50 employees
- **Tech/Business ratio:** ~40% / ~60%
- **Leadership depth:** Strong C-suite with CFO, COO, Head of Sales, Head of Ops

### Strengths

- **Deep fintech expertise:** Both founders from payment industry (HiPay, Intuit)
- **Elite education:** Polytechnique (COO), Centrale Nantes, HEC, Berkeley on the team
- **BNP Paribas backing:** Compliance support, commercial network, institutional credibility
- **Senior hires:** Ex-HiPay (Philippe Borie), ex-Alan (Marion Pigné), ex-Checkout (Armony Geter)
- **Dedicated compliance team:** Critical for EME license and marketplace operations
- **Tech infrastructure:** Multi-cloud expertise meeting BNP Paribas & ACPR requirements

### Areas of Concern

- **Team still small:** ~19 employees for European ambitions
- **High dependency on BNP Paribas:** Strategic partner but also potential constraint
- **Several shared/liaison roles:** Indicates startup-phase resource sharing
- **Young company:** Founded October 2023, still building track record

### Hiring Signals

Based on recent LinkedIn activity and announcements:
- **Philippe Borie** joined early 2025 (ex-HiPay) - strengthening technical team
- **Armony Geter** hired as Head of Operations (ex-Checkout) - scaling operations
- **Marion Pigné** joined March 2025 (ex-Alan) - building commercial function
- **Laurent Rousseau** promoted to CFO - financial structuring for growth

**Focus areas:** Operations, sales, technical leadership - typical Series A/growth stage hiring
